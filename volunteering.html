RAVE (Respite and Volunteer Experiences)

(As part of the Northern Nevada RAVE Family Foundation.) By far one of the most challenging and insightful volunteer experiences I've had. Helped take care of children with special needs for four hours, playing with them and keeping them out of trouble. The communication barrier was difficult to overcome - sometimes I wouldn't understand what they wanted, they would get angry for no reason, or they would ignore my instructions. Patience rewards all, though, and while I wouldn't ever want to go into early education, it was quite an insightful experience.
